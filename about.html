J. Nicholas Geist is an essayist, gamer, programmer, teacher, feminist (or at least an ally of feminists), all-around geek about town, and armchair narratologist.

He tweets about nonsense, writes about everything, and has entirely too much fun with web technologies.

Hereabouts, he marauds through videogames, nonfiction writing, life, media theory, and (as often as not) the Whedonverse, in pursuit of proof that these things are not as far apart as one might think.
